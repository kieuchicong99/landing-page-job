WELCOME TO VIETNAM

Vietnam is a beautiful and peaceful country located in Southeast Asia. Vietnam is one of the happiest and liveable countries in the world for foreigners, ranked with Switzerland, Singapore, Germany… According to a recent study 78% of percent of Vienamese people said that they are happy or very happy.

Vietnam is a country with a high preferential treatment for foreigners. Accordingly, 15% of foreigners working here earn more than 250,000 USD a year, higher than the global average of 7%. Vietnam is also considered a peaceful, politically stable and safe country to live and develop.

Vietnam is a country with a tropical monsoon climate, diverse and beautiful landscapes which are the reason why 15 million international tourists visit Vietnam every year. From rice fields to beaches and islands, Vietnamese’s nature is sparkling and stunning, it will make you happy inside.

Happiness is very hard to describe and to show you but you will feel it when you land in Vietnam.
